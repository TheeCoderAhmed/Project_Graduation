# Product

## Register

product

## Users
Patients in Turkey seeking reliable healthcare care, and practitioners (doctors, pharmacies) requiring streamlined workflows.

## Product Purpose
A healthcare review and rating mobile application that allows patients to search for providers, leave post-visit reviews, fill out structured questionnaires, and view ranked provider listings.

## Brand Personality
Professional, modern corporate, minimalistic, clinical precision balanced with human empathy. "Guided Confidence."

## Anti-references
Playful "pill-shaped" designs, heavy shadows, cluttered interfaces with excessive cognitive load, and traditional high-contrast action colors. Generic medical templates.

## Design Principles
- Guided Confidence: The interface should project stability and trust.
- Breathable Space: Use generous white space to reduce cognitive load.
- Tonal Depth: Avoid heavy shadows; use flat colors and subtle tonal layers.
- Clinical Precision: Use clear typography and distinct interactive elements.

## Accessibility & Inclusion
Generous line heights (1.5x-1.6x) for readability, distinct interactive focus states, high-contrast text on chips, and clear icons paired with text for alerts.
